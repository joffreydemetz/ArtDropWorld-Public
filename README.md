# ArtDrop World

ArtDrop World is a platform where artists hide physical artworks in real-world locations and invite people to find them through a mobile app.

It creates a bridge between art, urban exploration, and human connection — transforming everyday places into temporary, living galleries.

## Concept

ArtDrop is not a marketplace. It is an **experience**.

Artists — called **droppers** — hide their creations in the real world: a painting on a bench, a sketch in a doorway, a sculpture in a park. Each hidden artwork is called a **drop**.

Explorers — called **dropophiles** — use the app to discover nearby drops. They get approximate distances, hints, and difficulty levels. The rest is up to them.

Once found, the artwork can be:
- **Kept** — it's yours
- **Re-dropped** — hide it somewhere new to continue its journey
- **Left in place** — for someone else to discover

ArtDrop never claims ownership over artworks.

## How It Works

1. **Create** — An artist creates a drop with a description, hints, and artwork details
2. **Hide** — They place it in a real location and activate it on the map
3. **Explore** — Nearby dropophiles receive a notification and start searching
4. **Find** — The finder validates the discovery, rates the experience, and decides what to do next

## Philosophy

- Art first — art is experience, not content
- Exploration over consumption — the journey matters as much as the find
- Respect and connection — between artists, explorers, and public spaces
- Fairness — visibility is earned through quality, not payment
- Sustainable growth — at a human pace, serving creativity

## The Name

**Drop** as in "dropping" art into the world. Like a message in a bottle, but with a painting, a drawing, or a sculpture.

Art is hidden. You go find it. And something happens.

## Status

ArtDrop World is currently in **alpha**. Access is by invitation only.
